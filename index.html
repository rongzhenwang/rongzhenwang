Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in the GSAI-ML Group, Renmin University of China, advised by Prof. Chongxuan Li. Prior to PhD, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Statistics at Renmin University of China in 2023.

Currently, I am exploring the field of deep learning theory, with a core goal to understand and predict the behaviour of today's neural networks. I want to discover real phenomena and principles to inform and improve practice.

Previously, I have worked on topics in generalization theory and language model alignment.
